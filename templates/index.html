Our Events

Google Study Jam

With a crowd of 200+ we kick-started the year 2019 with the grand Google and Pydata Study Jam event. Students were given one month access to Google Cloud Platform through which they gained Google certification. The Pizzas were sponsored by Pydata for lunch and at the end we had a kahoot quiz for engagement of the crowd

Game Dev Workshop

It was a 2-day event on Game Development by Mr. Pravin Mane, a Senior Unity Game and Augmented Reality Developer. He gave the students a great idea about the basics on game development, how to start off with it. Participants got hands on implementation tutorial in workshop.
